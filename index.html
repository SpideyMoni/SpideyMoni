    About Spidey Moni

    OFILI DIVINE CHINEDU , also known as Spidey Moni, is a 19-year-old Nigerian rapper and artist. He is passionate about both music and football, chasing his dreams to become a successful footballer alongside his music career.

    The Trapstar Spidey Moni specializes in mimicking and remixing rap songs.

    Some of the rap songs he has remixed include:
    • NO FEELINGS (CHASES)
    • CHUKWUEBUKA (JERIQ)
    • NO EXCUSE (RORD KELLY)
    • DEAL WITH IT (PHYNO)
